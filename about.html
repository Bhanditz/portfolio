About Connor

Connor is the most prestigious Modern Artist in the world. Born in 2002, he created his first Modern Art piece at age two, by scribbling on the walls of his house.

At age 9, he had already made it into 14 Modern Art museums, and had already won 464 awards for his art.

After finally graduating college at age 17 with a PhD in Art, Dr. Connor went on to create some of today's most memorable pieces of art.
